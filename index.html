Inner City Youth Charitable Foundation

Our programs connect youth to mentors who serve as role models for careers, family life, and community service. Our mentorship programs focus on improving academic functioning, experiencing the value of work, expanding awareness and appreciation of self, others, and the environment beyond their home and neighborhood situations.
